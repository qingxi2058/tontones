Privacy 🔒

ToonTones respects your privacy.


• No account required. Play instantly, no sign-up.

• Scores stay local. Your scores are stored locally on your device only (localStorage).

• Basic analytics only. ToonTones uses Google Analytics to understand visits and improve the game. No account is required, and no score data is sent to us.


Your color memories stay yours. 🎨

How it works 🎨

A legendary color fills the screen. Your job: mix an exact match using three sliders.


H — Hue  The color family (red, cyan, violet…)

S — Saturation  How vivid vs. washed out

L — Lightness  Bright white to deep black


Hit Lock it in when you're confident. Score 0–10 based on proximity. Five rounds. One shot at glory.
