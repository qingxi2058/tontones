Game notes

A toon-tone style color game with its own name and feel.

TonTones is built for people who want that quick toon-tone style color guessing loop: see a shade, trust your memory, then mix it as close as you can. It stays close to the trend without taking the same name or pretending to be the same site.

  • Five-shade rounds are short enough to replay and compare.
  • HSL sliders make every guess feel deliberate, not random.
  • The result screen turns your guesses into a clean shareable score.
  • The brand stays TonTones, with its own domain and visual identity.
How it plays

Match five shades from memory

Each round shows a target shade and asks you to rebuild it with hue, saturation, and lightness. The final score shows how accurate your eyes were.

Why it works

Simple idea, strong replay pull

A good color memory game does not need a long tutorial. The question is immediate: do you actually remember the shade, or just the feeling of it?
